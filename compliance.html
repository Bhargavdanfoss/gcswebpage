    Compliance

    Extended Warranty

    Our team provides assistance for various production-related tasks, including supporting the Production Part Approval Process (PPAP), ensuring compliance with the Restriction of Hazardous Substances (RoHS) directive, aiding in production order management, offering guidance with Material Requirements Planning (MRP), and handling extended warranty services. They work to streamline these processes, ensuring that products meet quality standards, regulatory requirements, and customer expectations. Their efforts contribute to improved product quality, reduced risks, and enhanced overall efficiency in production and after-sales services.

    Trade Compliance

    Our team Supports trade compliance activities, ensuring adherence to regulations and policies governing international trade. They oversee import and export processes, monitor changes in trade laws, and ensure proper documentation and reporting. The team also conducts audits and assessments to identify areas for improvement and mitigate risks related to trade compliance. Their efforts help the organization maintain compliance, avoid penalties, and facilitate smooth international trade operations.

    Contract Management

    Our team Supports the management of contracts, which involves tasks such as drafting, negotiation, implementation, and monitoring of contractual agreements. They ensure that contracts meet legal requirements, align with business objectives, and mitigate risks. The team also maintains communication with stakeholders, tracks contract performance, and resolves any disputes that may arise. By managing contracts effectively, the team helps to optimize vendor relationships, control costs, and ensure compliance with contractual obligations.
